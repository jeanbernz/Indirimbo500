Privacy Policy

App Name: Indirimbo 500

Developer: Shine Rwanda

Last updated: June 13, 2026

1. Introduction

Indirimbo 500 is a Kinyarwanda SDA hymnal app developed by Shine Rwanda. This privacy policy explains how the app handles your information.

2. Data Collection

Indirimbo 500 does not collect, store, or share any personal information. The app does not require you to create an account or log in.

3. Internet Access

The app may access the internet only to stream hymn audio and load Solfège PDF files hosted on GitHub Pages. No personal data is sent or received during this process.

4. Offline Use

All hymn lyrics are stored locally within the app. The app can be used offline for reading lyrics without any internet connection.

5. Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

6. Children's Privacy

Indirimbo 500 is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children or any other users.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
